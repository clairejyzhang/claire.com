time: June 2024
---

This site is a personal archive to keep track of my writing. For me, writing = thinking. My nonfiction writing is where I process and internalize my experiences related to work/a future career. It also has the benefit of being something for potential employers to look at, but I don't want that to be the main purpose of this site. My creative writing is a way for me to process and explore my own memory. I'm interested in how recalling things through creative writing filters and changes those experiences.

The people who visit this site are probably going to fall into two different groups: 1) potential employer and 2) someone I know personally. If you're a potential employer and you're reading this, I hope you're liking what you're seeing so far. If you're someone I know personally, why are you stalking me 🤨
